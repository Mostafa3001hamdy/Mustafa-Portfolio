WhatsApp Business Card AI Agent

WhatsApp API Vision OCR HubSpot AWS Bedrock Google Drive Gmail
The brief

The team was collecting business cards at events and meetings, then losing them or manually retyping every field into HubSpot. The pipeline had to be frictionless — snap a photo, send it to a WhatsApp number, and let the system handle everything.

What I built
  • WhatsApp trigger that receives business card photos and routes them through Google Vision OCR for raw text extraction
  • LLM chain that parses extracted text into structured contact records (name, role, company, email, phone, website)
  • Automatic archive of the raw image to Google Drive and the parsed record to Google Sheets
  • HubSpot contact upsert with deduplication logic, then a personalized intro email queued via Gmail
  • Confirmation message back to the sender on WhatsApp — full loop closed in under a minute

Team Briefing Ingestion & Reporting Suite

n8n Jira Gmail API AI Agents Structured Output Drift Detection
The brief

Two teams — Software Development and Machine Learning — were running Monday briefings and Friday debriefs every week. Notes lived in Jira, commitments lived in email, drift went undetected. Leadership needed one unified, automated weekly view.

What I built
  • Monday briefing ingestion pulls Jira tickets, normalizes them, and runs an AI agent against a structured output parser to extract scope, owners, and risk signals

  • Midweek drift detection compares scope between Monday and Wednesday — classifies drift type (scope creep, risk increase, task count change) and routes a focused report
  • Separate report variants for the SD team and ML team with team-specific hierarchies and metrics

CS / 005

Commercial Pipeline Extraction & Follow-up Automation

n8n Gmail API AI Qualification Google Sheets WhatsApp
The brief

Real commercial opportunities were hiding inside two shared inboxes — buried under internal chatter, automated noise, and quoted reply chains. Leadership needed a way to surface every real deal, draft thoughtful follow-ups, and never let one go cold.

What I built
  • An extraction + qualification pipeline that mines access@ and tech@ inboxes, strips noise, and uses an AI agent to score and structure each thread into a deal record
  • Qualified opportunities are written to a central Google Sheet — one row per deal, with status, owner, stage, last touch, and next action
  • A draft-generation pass loops the sheet, sends each row through an LLM that writes a Gmail follow-up draft in the right tone for the deal stage
  • A weekly stale-deal sweep identifies opportunities that have gone quiet, drafts a re-engagement message, and pings leadership on WhatsApp when human action is required
  • End result: an always-on B2B sales pipeline driven entirely from the inbox, with zero manual qualification work

CS / 006

Historical Email Extraction & Validation Pipeline

n8n Gmail API Batch Processing HubSpot NeverBounce
The brief

The team needed to mine 15 months of Gmail history to rebuild the company contact graph — every real person we'd ever emailed with, deduped, validated, and ready for outreach. Doing it in one pass would crash n8n. Doing it manually was impossible.

What I built
  • Monthly batching strategy — the workflow processes one month at a time (Jan 2025 → Mar 2026), avoiding memory crashes and giving clean checkpoints to debug
  • Extracts every email address from From / To / CC / BCC / headers / body for maximum coverage
  • A blacklist + pattern engine drops noreply, mailer-daemon, UUID aliases, system traffic from HubSpot/Atlassian/Mixpanel, and internal accounts
  • Loop + Wait nodes keep API usage under rate limits; deduplication runs across the full corpus before any contact is committed
  • Clean contacts land in Google Sheets, get upserted into HubSpot, then validated through NeverBounce so only deliverable addresses make it into campaigns
