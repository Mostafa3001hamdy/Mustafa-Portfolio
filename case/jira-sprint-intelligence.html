01Overview

A suite that turns weekly briefings, debriefs, Jira sprints, and commitment emails into a single automated intelligence layer — extracting commitments, reconciling them against Jira, detecting drift, and emailing leadership RAG-status reports.

02Business problem

Two teams (Software Dev and ML) ran Monday briefings and Friday debriefs. Notes lived in Jira, commitments lived in email, and scope drift went undetected until it became a delivery problem. Leadership needed one automated, trustworthy weekly view.

06AI components

  • Multi-stage AI extraction of tasks/decisions/commitments with confidence scoring and speaker attribution.
  • Semantic-similarity matching of free-text commitments to Jira.
  • Drift classification into a DR1–DR8 taxonomy.

07Integrations

  • Jira — sprints, issues, comments.
  • Gmail — commitment emails + report delivery.
  • Google Sheets/Drive — baseline snapshots + storage.

08Technical challenges

  • Multi-stage extraction with confidence + speaker attribution.
  • Reconciling free-text commitments to Jira via semantic similarity (OK/MISMATCH/NO_MATCH).
  • Encoding governance as workflow logic — no delivery accepted on narrative alone; evidence required, approvals sequenced.
  • Idempotency so re-runs never double-post to Jira.

09Reliability engineering

  • Deployment: scheduled crons — Mon 18:00 & Fri 20:00 (Asia/Dubai), Tue 11:00 (Africa/Cairo).
  • Monitoring: meeting records with execution status + matched/missing counts per run.
  • Error handling: fail-fast on missing transcript, Jira retry, partial-success marking.
  • Validation: unique-ID idempotency and evidence checks.
  • Scalability: team-specific SD + ML variants with baseline-vs-actual snapshots and RAG roll-ups.

11Lessons learned

"Don't accept a spoken claim without evidence" became a rule I encoded directly into the pipeline. Automation isn't just faster reporting — it can enforce process discipline humans skip.

12Future improvements

  • Predictive drift alerts before Friday.
  • A live dashboard alongside the emailed report.
  • Auto-created Jira tickets for missing commitments.
