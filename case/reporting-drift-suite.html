Executive Reporting & Drift-Detection Suite

01The problem

Two teams (Software Dev and ML) ran Monday briefings and Friday debriefs. Notes lived in Jira, commitments lived in email, and scope drift went undetected until it became a delivery problem. Leadership needed one automated, trustworthy weekly view.

03Reconciliation workflow

Cron→Jira sprint→Gmail commitments→Normalize→Reconcile OK/MISMATCH/NO_MATCH→HTML report→Email leadership

04Integrations & AI components

  • APIs: Jira (sprints, issues, comments), Gmail (commitment emails + delivery), Google Sheets/Drive.
  • AI: multi-stage extraction of tasks/decisions/commitments with confidence scoring and speaker attribution; semantic-similarity matching; drift classification DR1–DR8.

05Technical challenges

  • Multi-stage AI extraction with confidence scoring and speaker attribution.
  • Reconciling free-text commitments against Jira via semantic similarity (OK/MISMATCH/NO_MATCH).
  • Encoding governance as workflow logic (no delivery accepted on narrative alone — evidence required, approvals sequenced).
  • Idempotency so re-runs never double-post to Jira.
