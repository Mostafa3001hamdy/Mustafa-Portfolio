Case Study 02 / Document AI · OCR · CRM

WhatsApp → CRM Business-Card Pipeline

Snap a business card, send it to a WhatsApp number, and it becomes a clean, deduplicated HubSpot contact in seconds — no manual re-typing, ever.

01The problem

The team collected business cards at events and meetings, then lost them or manually retyped every field into HubSpot. The pipeline had to be frictionless and reliable enough to trust with lead data.

03Workflow

WhatsApp trigger→Validate media→Vision OCR→LLM → JSON→Drive archive→Sheets→HubSpot upsert (dedup)→WhatsApp confirm

04Integrations & AI components

  • APIs: WhatsApp Business Cloud API, Google Vision OCR, Google Drive, Google Sheets, HubSpot.
  • AI: Google Vision OCR + LLM (AWS Bedrock) structured extraction to strict JSON (name, role, company, email, phone, website).

05Technical challenges

  • OCR output is noisy — an LLM after OCR normalizes raw text into strict, CRM-safe JSON.
  • Deduplication on email/phone so re-sent cards update rather than duplicate.
  • Explicit handling of every failure mode (missing image, OCR failure, malformed LLM output, CRM API error).

06Reliability engineering

Deployment
Self-hosted n8n · WhatsApp webhook (event-driven)
Monitoring

Error handling & retries
Media validation · graceful stop on no-image · branch on OCR/parse failure · CRM retry
Validation & scale
Dedup on email/phone · stateless per-message · concurrent senders

08Lessons learned

Chaining an LLM behind OCR is what turned an unreliable text dump into structured data safe to write to a CRM. Validation gates before every external write prevent silent corruption.
