WhatsApp AI Business-Card Agent

Snap a business card, send it to a WhatsApp number, and it becomes a clean, deduplicated HubSpot contact in seconds — no manual re-typing, ever.

01Overview

An event-driven pipeline that captures business cards over WhatsApp, extracts every field with OCR + an LLM, archives the image, and upserts a deduplicated contact into HubSpot — closing the loop with a WhatsApp confirmation in under a minute.

02Business problem

The team collected business cards at events and meetings, then lost them or manually retyped every field into HubSpot. The pipeline had to be frictionless and reliable enough to trust with lead data.

06AI components

  • Google Vision OCR for raw text extraction.
  • An LLM (AWS Bedrock) normalizes noisy OCR text into strict JSON (name, role, company, email, phone, website).

07Integrations

  • WhatsApp Business Cloud API — inbound card capture and confirmations.
  • Google Vision OCR — raw text extraction from the card image.
  • Google Drive — image archive.
  • Google Sheets — record of every card.
  • HubSpot — dedup upsert of the contact.
  • Gmail — optional intro email.

08Technical challenges

  • OCR output is noisy — an LLM after OCR is what makes it CRM-safe.
  • Deduplication on email/phone so re-sent cards update rather than duplicate.
  • Explicit handling of missing image, OCR failure, malformed LLM output, and CRM API errors.

09Reliability engineering

  • Deployment: self-hosted n8n, WhatsApp webhook (event-driven).
  • Monitoring: a Sheets record of every card + per-stage error logging.
  • Error handling: media validation, graceful stop on no-image, branch on OCR/parse failure, CRM retry.
  • Validation: required-field checks before the HubSpot write.
  • Scalability: stateless per-message processing handles concurrent senders.

11Lessons learned

Chaining an LLM behind OCR turned an unreliable text dump into structured data safe to write to a CRM. Validation gates before every external write prevent silent corruption.

12Future improvements

  • Add a phone-side confirmation preview before CRM write.
  • Support multi-card photos in one message.
  • Add a lightweight review queue for low-confidence extractions.
